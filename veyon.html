Privacy & Terms

What VEYON is. A learning app for Abacus & Vedic Maths. By creating an account you agree to use it respectfully.

Kids & consent. VEYON is intended for students. If you are under 13, a parent or guardian must agree on your behalf and consent to basic learning data (progress, scores) being stored to run the app.

Data. We store your username, learning progress, scores and purchases to provide the service. No passwords are stored in readable form. You can export or delete your data from the account menu.

Payments. Fees are paid manually and unlocked by an admin after verification. There is no automatic billing in the app.

Fair use. No abuse, cheating, or offensive usernames. Accounts may be locked or removed for violations.
